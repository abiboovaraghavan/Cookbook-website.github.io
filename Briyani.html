Chicken Briyani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish that is eaten in many countries with different styles. It is made with spices, rice, vegetables, or meat. Here are the steps to make biryani: 1. Wash the basmati rice. Before you begin cooking, you need to wash the rice. Fill a large bowl with cold water and pour in the rice. Use your hand to stir the rice in one direction. The water should get cloudy, so pour off the cloudy water. Refill the bowl with water. Continue rinsing the rice until the water is clear. Washing the rice removes surface starches and any debris. 2. Soak the rice. After you have rinsed the rice, soak it. Place the rice in a bowl of cold water and let it soak for 30 minutes to 2 hours ¹. 3. Chop the vegetables. If you are adding mixed vegetables, such as carrots, beans, tomatoes, cauliflower, or peas, cut them into smaller pieces. Make sure you wash the vegetables and set them aside, ready to be added to your rice. 4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon to the pan. Sauté for a few seconds, then add onions. Sauté until the onions turn transparent ¹. Add tomatoes and cashew nuts after the onions turn translucent. 5. Add mint leaves, coriander leaves, and green chilies to the pan ¹. Stir and sauté for a minute or so ¹. Then add ginger and garlic paste ¹. Stir into the mixture in the pan, then sauté for a couple of minutes. 6. Add garam masala, chili powder, carrots, peas and beans ¹. Sauté for a few more minutes, stirring regularly. 7. Pour in 8 cups of water ¹. After adding water, add salt to taste ¹. Stir everything together, then bring the mixture to a boil. 8. Add rice into boiling water ¹. Pour lemon juice into it ¹. Cover the pot with a tight lid ¹. Let the rice cook until done ¹. 9. The biryani is ready! Enjoy your delicious meal. I hope this helps!
